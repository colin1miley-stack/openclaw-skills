### B2B Marketplaces (Use with Caution)

| Platform | URL | Notes |
|----------|-----|-------|
| **Alibaba** | alibaba.com | Massive selection; heavy vetting required. Filter for "Gold Supplier" + "Verified." Request samples before any commitment. |
| **Thomasnet** | thomasnet.com | US-focused but some EU suppliers |
| **IndiaMART** | indiamart.com | Indian suppliers; often cheaper but longer lead times and import complexity |
| **Global Sources** | globalsources.com | Asia-focused; similar vetting rules as Alibaba |

**Alibaba vetting rules:**
- Only contact Gold Suppliers with 3+ years on platform
- Check "Verified Supplier" badge
- Request video call from their facility
- Never pay 100% upfront — use Alibaba Trade Assurance
- Order samples before any bulk order

### How to Use Trade Shows Without Attending

1. **Download the exhibitor list** — Most shows publish lists 4–6 weeks before the event
2. **Filter by category** — Look for "Contract Manufacturing," "Powder Processing," "Sachet Filling"
3. **Contact exhibitors directly** — Use the show as a warm intro: "I saw you listed as an exhibitor at Vitafoods and wanted to reach out..."
4. **Watch for post-show deals** — Some suppliers offer discounts to leads generated at shows

## UK Regulatory Bodies (for Verification)

| Body | What They Do | Website |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Food Standards Agency (FSA)** | UK food safety regulation; check for alerts or recalls | food.gov.uk |
| **MHRA** | Medicines and medical devices (not supplements, but useful boundary) | mhra.gov.uk |
| **Trading Standards** | Local enforcement; check for complaints | tradingstandards.uk |
| **Companies House** | UK company registration and filings | companieshouse.gov.uk |

**Verification workflow:**
1. Search Companies House for the supplier's company number
2. Check incorporation date, registered address, and filing history
3. Search the FSA website for any food alerts or recalls involving the company
4. Google "[company name] complaint" or "[company name] review"
