## Email Best Practices

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Send between 9am–11am Tuesday–Thursday | Send on Monday morning or Friday afternoon |
| Use the recipient's first name in the greeting | Use "Dear Sir/Madam" or "To Whom It May Concern" |
| Mention one specific thing about their company | Send a generic blast with zero customisation |
| Include a clear ask (call, quote, samples) | End with "Let me know if you're interested" — too vague |
| Keep it under 200 words | Write a novel |
| Follow up once, 4 days later | Spam with daily follow-ups |
| Track opens with a tool (Streak, Mailtrack) | Assume no response = no interest (follow up!) |

## Subject Line Alternatives (A/B Test)

If your open rates are low, try rotating these subject lines:

1. **"New supplement startup — powder sachet production"** (direct)
2. **"Quick question on minimum orders"** (low friction)
3. **"Vitafoods follow-up — [Your Brand]"** (if you met at a show)
4. **"Powder supplement partnership enquiry"** (professional)
5. **"Startup looking for long-term manufacturing partner"** (relationship-focused)
