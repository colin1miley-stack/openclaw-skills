# Competitor Monitoring Framework

## Overview

Track competitor activity in your market — pricing, product launches, marketing campaigns, SEO performance, and customer sentiment. Stay informed without obsessing.

## Alerts

### What to Alert Immediately
- Competitor launches product targeting your core demographic
- Competitor drops price below your floor
- Competitor raises funding / acquisition
- Competitor gets featured in major press
- New entrant in your exact niche

### What to Note Monthly
- General pricing trends
- Content strategy shifts
- New marketing channels
- Customer complaint patterns

## Response Rules

### When Competitor Drops Price
1. Do NOT immediately match — devalues brand
2. Check if it's temporary/promotional
3. Consider value-add instead (free shipping, bonus items)
4. Only match if it's sustained and hurting conversions

### When Competitor Launches Similar Product
1. Analyze their positioning and claims
2. Check compliance — are they making claims you can't?
3. Consider if you need to accelerate your roadmap
4. Update differentiation messaging

### When Competitor Gets Press Coverage
1. Note the angle — what resonated?
2. Consider pitching similar story to same outlet
3. Don't copy — find your own angle

## Ethical Boundaries

✅ **Do:**
- Monitor public information
- Learn from competitor strategies
- Differentiate based on genuine advantages
- Respond to market changes

❌ **Don't:**
- Scrape private data
- Spread misinformation
- Copy branding or messaging
- Poach employees
- File false complaints
