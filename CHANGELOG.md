## [0.1.0] — 2026-06-29

### Initial Release

The first public release of Colin Miley's OpenClaw Skills Collection — a curated set of high-quality skills for AI agents, built around growth marketing, e-commerce operations, and SEO automation. Every skill is designed to be clear, actionable, and immediately useful.

#### Growth & Marketing Funnel
- **cold-start-strategy** — Plan and execute cold start strategies for new products. Covers finding first users, launch channels, and zero-to-one growth tactics.
- **go-to-market** — Design go-to-market strategies, market entry plans, and positioning frameworks. Supports PLG, sales-led, and marketing-led motions.
- **growth-funnel** — Map and optimize the full AARRR pirate metrics funnel. Diagnose bottlenecks and plan actions across acquisition, activation, retention, revenue, and referral.
- **landing-page** — Create, optimize, and audit landing page content and structure. Covers hero sections, social proof, form optimization, and conversion-focused design.
- **homepage** — Build and optimize the primary site homepage. Covers hero, features, trust signals, and above-the-fold conversion.
- **copywriting** — Write and improve marketing copy for any page. Covers headlines, value propositions, CTAs, and persuasive messaging.
- **cta-component** — Design and optimize call-to-action buttons. Covers placement, copy, color psychology, and conversion-focused button design.
- **testimonials-component** — Create social proof and testimonial sections. Covers layout, credibility signals, and trust-building content.
- **pricing-page** — Design, structure, and optimize pricing pages. Covers tier strategy, anchoring, and comparison layouts.
- **about-page** — Write and optimize About page content. Covers company story, team, brand narrative, and authority building.
- **contact-page** — Create and optimize contact pages and forms. Covers form design, support channels, and inquiry handling.
- **lead-magnet** — Design and optimize lead magnet funnels. Covers opt-in offers, email capture, and value-first conversion.
- **page-cro** — Optimize specific page sections for conversion. Covers layout, copy, and UX testing at the page level.
- **conversion-strategy** — Improve conversion rates across the funnel. Covers A/B testing, friction reduction, and checkout optimization.
- **referral-program** — Design and implement referral marketing programs. Covers incentive structures, viral loops, and referral tracking.
- **retention-strategy** — Improve customer retention and reduce churn. Covers cohort analysis, re-engagement, and loyalty tactics.

#### Paid Advertising
- **google-ads** — Set up, optimize, and manage Google Ads campaigns. Covers Search, Display, YouTube, and Performance Max.
- **meta-ads** — Plan and execute Meta (Facebook/Instagram) ad campaigns. Covers audience targeting, creative strategy, and funnel optimization.
- **linkedin-ads** — Create and optimize LinkedIn advertising campaigns. Covers B2B targeting, lead gen forms, and sponsored content.
- **reddit-ads** — Plan and execute Reddit advertising campaigns. Covers subreddit targeting, community engagement, and ad creative.

- **pinterest-marketing** — Plan and execute Pinterest marketing strategies. Covers visual SEO, Rich Pins, and shopping campaigns.
- **paid-ads-strategy** — Plan overall paid advertising mix and budget allocation. Covers channel selection, attribution, and ROI optimization.
- **ai-traffic** — Track and analyze AI search traffic in GA4 and GSC. Covers ChatGPT, Perplexity, and AI Overview referral tracking.
